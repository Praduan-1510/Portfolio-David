## About

**One-liner** (teasers / meta):
UI/UX and graphic designer based in West Bengal, India — focused on clarity, usability, and strong visual systems.

**About — long (about page):**

I'm a UI/UX and graphic designer with 3+ years of experience spanning product interfaces, brand and marketing design, and instructional content. I like the part of design where a messy, complex idea becomes something clear, usable, and good-looking — whether that's a mobile flow, a landing page, or a full visual system.

My path ran from content and instructional design into UI/UX, so I think as much about structure and information as I do about how something looks. I work primarily in Figma — wireframing, prototyping, and building reusable components — and I care about hierarchy, accessibility, and shipping work that actually supports the goal behind it.

`[confirm — keep if you like the angle]` I studied English (Honours) at Amity University, which still shapes how I approach design: reading closely, structuring information, and communicating clearly.

**Approach (3 principles):**
- **Clarity first.** Make the complex easy to understand and use — structure and hierarchy before decoration.
- **Systems, not one-offs.** Reusable components and a consistent visual language, so the work scales and stays coherent.
- **Design with a purpose.** Every screen serves a real user need and a real goal, not just an aesthetic.
